01 · Brutalist · LIVE

Bold & type-forward
Pure black-on-white with one electric red accent. Massive Helvetica, hard rectangles, no rounded corners, asymmetric grid lines. This is the current home page.
On the shelf FUTURE PICKS

02 · Terminal

Phosphor & ASCII
Dark green-on-black CRT terminal with amber highlights. Monospace everything, ASCII bordered cards, blinking cursor, scanlines.

03 · Cinema Marquee

Theatrical & ornate
Black + gold + velvet with light-bulb frame and Bodoni Moda italics. Each deck a "feature presentation". Old playbill vibe.

04 · Glitch / Cyberpunk

Neon & chromatic
Deep violet with neon pink + cyan. Chromatic-aberration logo, gradient buttons, clipped-corner cards. Very online energy.

05 · Newspaper

Op-ed broadsheet
Cream paper with classic Playfair + Garamond. Dateline, multi-column articles, drop caps, double rules. Times-style elegance.

06 · Transit Map

Wayfinding & routes
Bold colored "lines" connecting decks like an underground map. Station cards, route badges, NYC Subway / TfL energy.

07 · Blueprint

Technical drawing
Deep blue with cyan grid lines, title blocks, dimensions, drawing labels. Architect's working set aesthetic.

08 · Polaroid Scrapbook

Analog & handwritten
Kraft paper desk with polaroid photos, washi tape, handwritten Caveat captions. Personal scrapbook warmth.

09 · SaaS Iris

Premium product landing
Soft white with iris/violet primary + orange-gradient logo. Sticky header, big serif-ish hero, floating document cards tilted at angles. Inspired by resumefilter.vercel.app (Hakim El Hattab — creator of Reveal.js).

10 · Score Rings

Dashboard with scoring
Slate dashboard with each deck shown as a candidate card — circular progress ring with numeric score (depth/freshness), status pills, tags, hover-rotating open button. Same ResumeFilter DNA, focused on the candidate-list pattern.
