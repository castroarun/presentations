05 · Built withQuantifyd
How to read a backtest

P/L is the hook. The numbers below decide if you can live with it.

CAGR
Compound annual growth. Smooths a great year against a terrible one — apples to apples.
Max drawdown
Worst peak-to-trough drop. This is what breaks people — the average means nothing if you capitulate at the bottom.
Calmar
CAGR ÷ Max DD. Return per unit of pain. Above 1 is fine, above 2 is rare — the honest way to compare two strategies.
Sharpe
Return per unit of volatility. Industry convention — treats upside and downside the same, which a trader doesn't.
Profit factor
Gross wins ÷ gross losses. Above 1.5 decent, above 2 strong. Tells you if the edge survives costs and slippage.
Win rate
Misleading alone. 80% wins still loses if the losses are big — only useful next to PF and average R.

The same +100% means very different things with 10% max DD and 60% max DD.

13 · MQ — HeadlineQuantifyd
MQ · three lessons from a thousand runs

Concentration is the only lever.

PS10 → 49% CAGR  ·  PS15 → 38%  ·  PS30 → 32%. Everything else is noise.

Quality weights change nothing.

Every fundamental re-weight I tried picked the same thirty stocks. Ranking stabilised a long time ago.

Technical filters hurt.

EMA, RSI, SuperTrend, MACD, ADX — every overlay dragged CAGR down. The ATH-drawdown exit already does the job of every hard stop I wrote.

15 · MQ + V3Quantifyd
● Disabled 2026-04-01  ·  Maruthi

Pulled in live. Nine bugs later,
I still haven't re-enabled it.

Dual SuperTrend regime on MARUTI 30-min candles, with option hedges on breaches. The idea is good. The implementation lived through every class of bug a live system can have.

  • auth   TOTP login needed multi-hop redirects · token expired silently mid-session
  • contract   Hardcoded lot size drifted from Kite's instruments API · sized wrong
  • indicator   ATR used simple mean · TradingView uses Wilder's RMA · signals off
  • orders   Option legs on MARKET · needed LIMIT with fill-check-revise retry
  • state   Stale regime served from DB · not reconciled against live computation
  • races   Duplicate option orders on tick bursts · needed position-count guard

Rule: a strategy doesn't come back live until every failure mode is in the backtest harness.
