01 · Brutalist

Bold & type-forward
Pure black-on-white with one electric red accent. Massive Helvetica, hard rectangles, no rounded corners, asymmetric grid lines. Reads like a Pentagram / Massimo Vignelli design system. Confident, loud, type-driven.

02 · Terminal

Phosphor & ASCII
Dark green-on-black like a CRT terminal, with amber highlights. Monospaced everything, ASCII-bordered cards, blinking cursor, scanline overlay. Hacker / engineer aesthetic — feels like ssh-ing into a personal site.

03 · Cinema Marquee

Theatrical & ornate
Black + gold + velvet red, with lightbulb-edged marquee frame and Bodoni Moda serif italics. Each deck is a "feature" with a poster card. Old playbill / theatre programme vibe — romantic and ceremonial.

04 · Glitch / Cyberpunk

Neon & chromatic
Deep violet with neon pink + cyan. Chromatic-aberration logo, grid-line background, gradient buttons, clipped-corner cards. Y2K-meets-2026 vibe — high energy, very online, feels demo-ready for an AI talk.
